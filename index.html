Welcome to Call's Life!

"Call’s life" is an interactive game where you step into the shoes of Call, a teen navigating life after his best friend's tragic loss to drug abuse. Facing tough choices, you'll decide Leo's path through moments of grief, peer pressure, and the temptation of drugs. This game isn't just a story—it's a journey that sheds light on the real challenges and decisions teens face regarding substance abuse. By making choices for Call, you'll see firsthand the impact of these decisions, not just on him, but his friends too. "Call’s life" aims to spark conversation and awareness about drug abuse, showing how choices can lead to different paths. It's a powerful reminder that we're not alone in our struggles and that our decisions matter. Short, impactful, and relatable, this game is designed to resonate with teens, encouraging them to think critically about the choices they face.
